About me

My name is Ana Chicaiza and I am 26 years old. I was born in Quito, Ecuador but my family moved to Spain when I was 3 years old. When I was 19 I decided London was a good place to start working and improve my English. My first job was as a waitress. I had already been living in London for 2 years when I had the opportunity to apply for university. It was the best decision in my life. I am in my second year of a degree called BSc Applied Computing. She Codes workshops have helped me a lot to understand coding and I have also found it very entertaining and didactic which is what I needed because sometimes it is very difficult for me to concentrate.

I love reading and one of my favorites books is "Hundred years of solitude" by Gabriel Garcia Marquez. Tim Burton is my favorite film and one of my favorite films is "Nightmare Before Christmas" and "The Corpse Bridge". When I was 7 years old I loved to play football but when I got older I switched to basketball. I don't play every day but I usually get together with my friends to play every other weekend. I prefer summer to winter and a walk in the mountains to a walk on the beach. I see more beauty in cloudy days than in sunny days, although it is true that sunny days bring more happiness.
